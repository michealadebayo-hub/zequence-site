# Sample Proposal - Small Retail Shopify Store

## Project Overview

Client: Example Small Retail Business

Project: Shopify Store for Clothes, Jewellery and Cooking Utensils

Date:

Prepared by: Michael Adebayo, Zequence Digital UK

## Summary

Thank you for the opportunity to support your business.

Based on the product mix and the goal of selling online in a clear, professional way, the recommended approach is a Growth Shopify Store. This gives the business a strong structure for multiple product categories while keeping the store easy to manage and ready to grow.

## Goals

- Launch a professional online store
- Make it easy for customers to browse across different product categories
- Create a smooth mobile-friendly shopping experience
- Set the business up on a platform that is simple to manage over time

## Recommended Scope

### Included

- Shopify store setup
- Homepage design
- Collection/category structure
- Product page setup
- About page
- Contact page
- FAQ or policy pages
- Payment setup
- Shipping setup
- Mobile optimisation

### Suggested Store Structure

- Clothes
- Jewellery
- Cooking Utensils

### Recommended Tier

Growth Shopify Store

Suggested pricing direction: from GBP 2,000

Final quote depends on:

- Product count
- Number of collections
- Whether product uploads are included
- App or integration needs
- Content readiness

## Suggested Product Scope

- Up to 30 products
- Up to 8 collections
- Clean homepage and collection layout
- Essential Shopify setup and launch support

## Optional Add-Ons

- Extra product uploads
- Product description support
- Review app setup
- Discount or promotional setup
- Email sign-up integration
- Ongoing support after launch
