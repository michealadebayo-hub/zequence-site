# Discovery Call Checklist - Shopify Lead (Small Retail)

Use this for a small retail business selling mixed product categories such as clothes, jewellery, and cooking utensils.

## Business Basics

- What does the business sell?
- How long has the business been operating?
- Is this the first online store or a redesign?

## Product Scope

- How many products are ready for launch?
- What categories or collections are needed?
- Will the product range grow soon after launch?
- Do they already have product photos, pricing, and descriptions?

## Sales Process

- Do they want customers to pay online immediately?
- Do they need delivery, local collection, or both?
- Do they need discount codes or promotions?

## Store Experience

- What kind of look and feel do they want?
- Are there any websites they like?
- Do they want a simple store or something more polished and brand-led?

## Setup Needs

- Do they already have a Shopify account?
- Do they have a logo and brand colours?
- Do they need help uploading products?
- Do they need help with shipping and payment setup?

## Timeline And Budget

- When do they want to launch?
- Is there a key event or deadline?
- What budget range are they working with?

## Recommendation Notes

Use this section after the call:

- Starter Shopify Store if the catalog is small and the setup is simple
- Growth Shopify Store if they need better category structure and a more polished store
- Custom Shopify quote if the scope is larger, more complex, or requires more integrations
