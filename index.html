Why Protocol 14 Matters

Protocol 14 introduces structured expectations for clinical AI safety, governance oversight, and audit traceability across healthcare environments.

Organisations deploying AI-assisted tools in patient-facing workflows will be expected to demonstrate human oversight controls, documentation verification processes, and insurer-aligned risk frameworks.

Early preparation reduces regulatory exposure, mitigates liability risk, and strengthens procurement positioning.
