# Pre-Launch Checklist - eBay To Shopify Client

Use this to guide the client before launch.

## Business Setup

- Confirm business name
- Confirm contact email
- Confirm best phone / WhatsApp
- Decide whether a domain is being purchased now

## Branding

- Logo ready or basic logo direction agreed
- Brand colours chosen
- Any reference websites collected

## Products

- Product list confirmed
- Product photos ready
- Product descriptions ready
- Prices confirmed
- Categories / collections agreed

## Store Setup

- Shopify account created
- Payment gateway ready
- Shipping rules confirmed
- Delivery or collection options decided
- Policy pages prepared

## Final Launch Readiness

- Homepage approved
- Product pages approved
- Mobile view checked
- Test payment completed
- Contact details confirmed
