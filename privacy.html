Privacy Policy

How we handle website enquiries.

Last updated: 27 May 2026

Who We Are

This website is operated by Zequence Digital UK Limited, a UK software company. You can contact us at hello@zequencedigital.co.uk.

Information We Collect

When you use the contact form, we collect the name, email address, and message you choose to send. If you contact us by WhatsApp or email, we receive the information included in that message.

How We Use It

We use enquiry information to reply to you, understand your request, provide product or service information, and keep a record of business conversations.

Form Processing

The website contact form is processed through FormSubmit so that messages can be sent to our email inbox. FormSubmit may process submitted form data to deliver the enquiry.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand general website usage, such as page visits and enquiry interactions. Analytics information helps us improve the website.

How Long We Keep It

We keep enquiry records for as long as needed to respond, manage the relationship, and meet reasonable business or legal requirements.

Your Choices

You can ask us to update or delete your enquiry information by emailing hello@zequencedigital.co.uk.
