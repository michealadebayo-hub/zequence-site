# Quote Guide - eBay To Shopify Client

Use this when quoting a client who currently sells on eBay but does not yet have a domain, branding, payment gateway, or structured website.

## Recommended Positioning

This is not just a store design project.

It is a guided Shopify launch that helps the client move from marketplace selling into their own branded online presence.

## Suggested Pricing Direction

### Starter Guided Shopify Launch

From GBP 1,500

Use this if:

- product count is small
- brand needs are light
- the client mainly needs setup and launch guidance

### Growth Guided Shopify Launch

From GBP 2,000

Use this if:

- product categories need better structure
- the client needs more design polish
- the client needs more setup support
- there are more products or more content needs

### Quote Higher If Needed

Increase the quote if the client needs:

- logo or brand support
- product upload support
- copywriting help
- large catalog setup
- app integrations
- migration complexity

## Suggested Scope

- Shopify account guidance
- Domain guidance
- Basic branding direction
- Homepage and store page design
- Product and collection setup
- Payment setup
- Shipping setup
- Basic policies
- Mobile optimisation
- Handover support
