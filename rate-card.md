# Zequence Digital UK Rate Card

## Website And Digital Service Pricing

These are starting prices for Zequence Digital UK projects. Final pricing depends on scope, timeline, features, and content requirements.

### Starter Website

From GBP 500

Ideal for:

- New businesses
- Personal brands
- Service businesses that need a clean and professional online presence
- Smaller businesses that want a strong starting point without a larger custom scope

Typical inclusions:

- Core pages
- Mobile-friendly design
- Contact form setup
- Basic on-page SEO setup

### Business Website

From GBP 950

Ideal for:

- Businesses that need more structure, stronger messaging and a more polished user journey

Typical inclusions:

- More pages and sections
- Stronger content structure
- Conversion-focused design
- Contact and enquiry flow setup

### Shopify Store

From GBP 1,500

Ideal for:

- Fashion brands
- Jewellery businesses
- Product-based businesses selling online

Typical inclusions:

- Product and collection pages
- Shopping and checkout flow
- Mobile optimisation
- Better customer journey design
- Shopify setup tailored to your brand

#### Shopify Tier Guide

##### Starter Shopify Store

From GBP 1,500

Suggested scope:

- Up to 10 products
- Up to 3 collections
- Core store pages
- Basic payment and shipping setup
- Mobile-friendly theme customisation

##### Growth Shopify Store

From GBP 2,000

Suggested scope:

- Up to 30 products
- Up to 8 collections
- More polished homepage structure
- App setup for key features
- Stronger product and collection experience

##### Custom Shopify Store

From GBP 3,000+

Suggested scope:

- 30+ products
- Advanced store structure
- More tailored design requirements
- Extra integrations, apps, or migration support
- Larger catalog or more complex product setup

##### Common Shopify Add-Ons

- Extra product uploads
- Additional collection setup
- Product description support
- Review app or upsell app setup
- Email sign-up or marketing integration
- Migration from another platform

### Automation And Software

From GBP 350

Ideal for:

- Small business workflow automations

More advanced software or custom automation projects are quoted after discovery.
