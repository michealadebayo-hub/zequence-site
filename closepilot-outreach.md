# ClosePilot — Outreach Messages

Short, copy-paste templates for booking ClosePilot Early Access demos.
Landing page: https://zequencedigital.co.uk/closepilot.html

**Tips**
- Personalise the first line per firm (mention their accounting software or a recent pain point).
- Send in small batches so you can follow up properly.
- Always end with one clear ask: *"Open to a 20-minute demo next week?"*
